THE GAMES

TEAMS OF 3 ARE REQUIRED TO PLAY.

- TEAM TROUBLE

Teams are required to work together to escape 3 rooms in total. You have 5 minutes to complete this.

- SCORING

1st Place - 100 Points

2nd Place - 90 Points

3rd Place - 80 Points

4th Place - 70 Points

Others - 50 Points if completed.

- PARKOUR MANIA

Complete the parkour. Checkpoints will be there every few sections. You have 5 minutes to complete this.

- SCORING

1st Place - 100 Points

2nd Place - 90 Points

3rd Place - 80 Points

4th Place - 70 Points

Others - 50 Points if completed.

- SKYWARIAL

Teams are placed on differnt islands. There are islands with tools and loot. It is a fight to the death. There is a shrinking border. You have 20min to complete this.

- SCORING

A kill - 50 Points

Survive till the end - 200 Points

Survive half of the game - 100 Points
